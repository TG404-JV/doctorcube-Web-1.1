Our Story

Welcome to DoctorCubes Travel Education Abroad

We are your trusted consultancy for pursuing MBBS abroad with 6+ years of experience in the field of international medical education. At DoctorCubes, we specialize in guiding Indian students to top medical universities in Russia and other countries around the world.

Our mission is to make quality medical education accessible and affordable for aspiring doctors from India. With our extensive network and exclusive partnerships with over 12 Russian universities, we provide end-to-end support for students looking to fulfill their dreams of becoming medical professionals.

What sets us apart is our personalized approach to each student's journey. From the initial counseling session to post-admission support, we ensure that every aspect of studying abroad is taken care of, allowing students to focus on their education and personal growth.
